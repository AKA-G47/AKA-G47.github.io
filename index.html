About Me

I'm a Ph.D student in Applied Mathematics at the University of Massachusetts Amherst under the suppervision of Matthew Dobson. Before starting my Ph.D, I completed a MS in Applied Mathematics at UMass Amherst.


Research Interests

My research interests are in Statistical Mechanics, Nonequilibrium Molecular Dynamics, and Machine Learning.
